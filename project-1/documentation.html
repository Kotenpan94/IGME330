Proposals

The major features of my app will allow users to search up a given Genshin Impact character and learn what items they need to build said character. Users will also be able to favorite their searches so that they can come back to the website in the future in case they need to check it out again. Finally, there will be a way to like a character, as a way to see what characters are the most popular, which might also make other users more tempted to build said characters.
